About Peak web Designs

Hand-Coded Websites That Actually Grow Your Business

Hey there! I'm Williams, and I'm probably different from every other web designer you've talked to. While most developers are busy dragging and dropping with WordPress or Wix, I'm over here hand-coding every single website from scratch. Why? Because I believe your business deserves better than a cookie-cutter template that looks like everyone else's. I got into web development because I was tired of seeing small businesses get ripped off by agencies charging thousands for slow, generic WordPress sites that get hacked every other month. You know the ones - they take forever to load, look like they were designed in 2010, and cost a fortune to maintain. I thought, "There has to be a better way."

So I learned to code websites the right way - by hand, from the ground up. No shortcuts, no bloated code, no security vulnerabilities. Just clean, fast, custom websites that actually work to grow your business. And instead of charging you thousands upfront (because let's be honest, most small businesses don't have $5,000 sitting around), I offer flexible payment options that won't break your budget. What I found is that business owners are stuck in a frustrating spot. They either can't afford a professional website, or if they can, they don't know who to trust to build something that actually works. Most end up with expensive disappointments that don't bring in leads. That's exactly the problem I solve.
